Queue Bundling Orders

Queue bundling orders for the MultiTerm XML to TBX Converter are a means of grouping related elements into the appropriate groupings for TBX, which often requires grouping elements, such as descripGrp, to wrap around ordinary data categories to associate them with their subordinates.

For example, in MultiTerm XML:

A group of stars formed together in the spiral arms of a galaxy. Oxford2007

In TBX, this would be:

A group of stars formed together in the spiral arms of a galaxy. Oxford2007

Orders are executed in the sequence listed, so later orders can drain queues filled by earlier ones if necessary. In the Converter, you select each queue bundling order by clicking “Add” on the Queue Bundling Orders window and using the queue bundling tool
